Quick DNA Extraction Protocol

PHENOL-CHLOROFORM EXTRACTION

1. Add tissue to lysis buffer (10mM Tris, 10mM NaCl)
2. Add Proteinase K (25µL of 20mg/mL)
3. Incubate at 55°C for 4 hours
4. Add equal volume phenol:chloroform:IAA (25:24:1)
5. Vortex 30 sec, centrifuge 10,000g for 10 min at 4°C
6. Transfer aqueous layer
7. Repeat phenol extraction until interface clear
8. Add 1 volume ice-cold isopropanol
9. Spool DNA with glass rod
10. Rinse with 70% ethanol, air dry
11. Resuspend in TE buffer overnight at 4°C
